Our Story

Started with a Dream,
Built on Trust

DCA Car Rentals was born out of a personal frustration — overpriced, unreliable rental services with hidden fees and no transparency. Dhanush set out to change that.

Today, DCA Car Rentals operates across Karnataka and beyond, offering a curated fleet of India's finest vehicles — from the rugged Mahindra Thar to the luxurious Toyota Innova Crysta.

We believe every journey should be comfortable, safe, and memorable — whether you're on a weekend getaway, a business trip, or a family road trip.
