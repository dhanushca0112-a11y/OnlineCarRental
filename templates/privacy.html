2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your car rental bookings.
  • To verify payments and maintain billing records.
  • To send booking confirmations, receipts, and important service updates via email/SMS.
  • To provide 24/7 roadside assistance and emergency support.
  • To operate women's safety features including SOS alerts and live GPS tracking.
  • To improve our website, app, and services based on usage patterns.
  • To comply with Indian legal and regulatory requirements.
  • To send occasional promotional offers (only if you opt in).

We do not use your data for automated profiling or sell it to any third party for marketing purposes.

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Assigned Drivers: Your name and phone number are shared with the driver for "With Driver" bookings.
  • Emergency Services: In a safety emergency, your location and name may be shared with police or medical services.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required by Indian law, court order, or government authority.
  • Safety Contacts: Your live location is shared with contacts you personally nominate in the safety feature.

We do not share your information with advertisers, data brokers, or marketing companies.

4. Cookies & Tracking

DCA Car Rentals uses minimal, essential cookies only:

  • Session cookies: Keep you logged in during your visit. Deleted when you close the browser.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your dark/light mode choice. Stored in localStorage.
  • No advertising or tracking cookies are used by DCA Car Rentals.

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking scripts.

5. Data Security

  • All passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (Werkzeug/bcrypt). We cannot see your password.
  • Data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database on secured servers.
  • All web traffic is encrypted via HTTPS/SSL.
  • Access to customer data is restricted to Dhanush and authorised DCA staff only.
  • Regular security audits are conducted to identify and fix vulnerabilities.

While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% immune. In case of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours.

6. Your Rights (Indian IT Act & DPDP Act)

Under Indian data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format (CSV/JSON).
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data for marketing purposes.
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact Dhanush at dhanush@dcacarrentals.com or call +91 6362948259. Requests will be processed within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

DCA Car Rentals services are intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we become aware that a child under 18 has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately. Contact us at +91 6362948259 if you believe we have inadvertently collected such information.

8. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained as long as your account is active.
  • Booking records: Retained for 3 years for legal and tax compliance.
  • Deleted accounts: Data anonymised within 30 days of deletion request.
  • Safety dashcam recordings: Deleted after 72 hours unless an incident is reported.
